Services

individual therapy

We offer individual therapy for young and emerging adults, specifically women in their twenties and thirties who are struggling with a variety of concerns such as perfectionism, anxiety, depression, low self-esteem, body image concerns, disordered eating, relationship issues, OCD, transitions, and school/work stress. Our therapy sessions provide a safe and non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Through a combination of evidence-based techniques and a collaborative approach, we will work together to identify and address the underlying issues that may be holding you back from achieving your goals and living a fulfilling life. Our approach is tailored to your unique needs and goals, with a focus on helping you develop skills and strategies to manage your symptoms and improve your overall well-being. Our sessions will help you gain clarity, build resilience, and achieve greater balance in your life. This is a compassionate and non-judgmental environment where you can feel comfortable expressing your concerns and working towards meaningful change.

Supervision for pre and fully-licensed clinicians

Whether you are pursuing clinical licensure or simply want a space to continue your growth as a fully-licensed clinical. Mollie Spiesman, LCSW provides supervision to social workers and mental health counselors. We are here to provide you with the guidance, support, and training you need to succeed in your career. Our supervision sessions are tailored to meet your unique needs and goals, and are designed to help you develop your clinical skills, deepen your understanding of theory and research, and enhance your overall professional confidence and competence. Supervision is a powerful tool for personal and professional growth, and we are honored to support you on your journey.
