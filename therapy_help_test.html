Areas of Focus

Anxiety

Anxiety includes excessive worry, fear and unease that can impact daily life. (some examples of anxiety disorders include Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Separation-Anxiety, Social Anxiety, Panic Attacks).

We will work to identify and challenge anxious thoughts and behaviors using evidence-based techniques such as CBT and mindfulness.

Low Self-Esteem

Low self-esteem can negatively impact how we view ourselves and our capabilities.

We’ll explore the root causes of your self-doubt, identify and challenge negative self-talk, and help you build self-compassion and self-confidence.

Perfectionism

Perfectionism can be a double-edged sword, motivating us to strive for excellence but also causing anxiety and self-criticism due to an excessive need for control and the desire to be perfect in every aspect of life.

I support clients in developing self-awareness and compassion surrounding perfectionistic tendencies, setting realistic and flexible goals, and boundaries for balance.

Disordered Eating

Disordered eating can be a complex issue related to self-esteem, body image, and emotional regulation that includes rules around food and movement.

We’ll work to explore the underlying factors contributing to your behaviors, build a healthier relationship with food, movement, and your body and develop coping strategies using CBT and intuitive eating techniques.

Stressful Life Transitions

Stressful life transitions can be overwhelming, whether it's starting college, changing jobs, or ending a relationship.

I help you navigate these transitions by providing support, tools for self-care, and coping strategies to manage stress and build resilience.

Relationship Issues

Relationship issues can cause emotional distress and impact our well-being.

We’ll work to identify and address patterns of communication and behavior that may be contributing to your struggles, build healthy boundaries, and develop skills for effective communication and conflict resolution.

Body Image Concerns

Body image concerns can negatively impact self-esteem and lead to disordered eating, anxiety, and depression.

We will explore the root causes of your negative body image, build self-compassion, and develop strategies to improve body acceptance while challenging societal and personal beliefs about beauty and worth.

Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) is a challenging condition characterized by intrusive thoughts and repetitive behaviors that interferes with daily life.

I utilize Exposure and Response Prevention (EXRP)- the gold standard for OCD treatment that involves gradually exposing clients to anxiety-provoking stimuli and preventing compulsive behaviors, ultimately reducing anxiety and improving functioning.

School and/or Work Stress

School and work stress can cause anxiety and impact mental health

We’ll work together to manage stress and build resilience by exploring coping strategies, setting realistic goals, and improving time management and organization skills.
