FAQs

  • How does therapy work?

    We’ll start with a complimentary 15-minute video consultation. If it is a mutual fit, we’ll set up an initial appointment. During this session, I’ll ask you more detailed questions in order to get to know you better. We will create a treatment plan that’s tailored to you and what you are looking to get out of therapy. We will then meet at a mutually agreed upon frequency (typically weekly) to work toward your goals.

  • What can I expect from my consultation?

    Consultations are 15 minutes and are held over video. This allows us to see if we might be a good fit for each other, and gives you the opportunity to ask any questions you may have. We will also talk about what you hope to get out of therapy, and logistics such as billing and scheduling.

  • Do you offer in-person therapy?

    All sessions are being held virtually through our secure video platform at this time.

  • How long and how often is therapy?

    Therapy sessions are typically 45 minutes long. Therapy yields the most consistent results when attended on a weekly basis. It is an ongoing process and there may be times where you will need to be seen more or less frequently.

    There are no rules for how long you should be in therapy for. Some clients come to therapy short-term to resolve a specific issue and others remain in therapy for years for continued support and growth. We will work together to determine when your therapeutic goals have been met.

  • What are the benefits of virtual therapy?

    Virtual therapy offers a convenient and flexible option for receiving therapy from the comfort of your own home or any other location that works for you. With virtual therapy, there's no need to deal with the stress and hassle of commuting to appointments, and it makes it easier to fit therapy into your busy schedule. We conduct virtual appointments through a secure video platform that's accessible via our client portal, providing you with a confidential and private setting to receive the support and care you need.

  • Do you accept insurance?

    Mollie Spiesman LCSW PLLC is considered an out-of-network provider with all insurances. As an out-of-network provider, we are committed to helping you receive the maximum benefits available through your out-of-network mental health coverage. If you are looking to receive reimbursement from your insurance, we can provide you with a superbill that includes all of the necessary information for self-claim submission. Alternatively, we offer claim submission services to help facilitate this reimbursement process.

  • Why don’t you accept insurance directly?

    Mollie Spiesman LCSW PLLC operates as an out-of-network therapy practice. By not accepting insurance directly, we are able to focus solely on serving our clients. If we were to accept insurance, we would be subject to their regulations, including the possibility of limited coverage and less privacy in regards to personal health information. As an out-of-network provider, we are committed to working directly with you, with greater flexibility and confidentiality in our approach to therapy.

  • What should I ask my insurance carrier to understand my out-of-network coverage?

    Call the number on the back of your insurance card and ask the following questions:

    • Does my plan offer any out-of-network benefits for mental health?
    • Do I have an out-of-network deductible? If yes, how much of it has already been met this year?
    • Are there any limitations on the number of sessions my plan covers for mental health?
    • Are virtual outpatient therapy sessions covered by my plan?
    • How much will I be reimbursed per visit when I see an out-of-network therapist?
    • Do I need prior approval or a referral in order to receive reimbursement for out-of-network therapy?
    • How long does the process take from when the claim is submitted to when I would receive reimbursement?
  • How does payment work?

    Clients are responsible for payment of the session fee at the time of the session. We accept payment by credit card or HSA/FSA cards. Your card is stored securely in your client portal. Additionally, all billing documents are available to you in your client portal for your convenience.
